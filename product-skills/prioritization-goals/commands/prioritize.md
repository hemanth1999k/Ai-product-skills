# /prioritize

Score and rank product initiatives using the right framework for your context.

## Usage
`/prioritize [list of items]`
`/prioritize rice [list of items]`
`/prioritize weighted [list of items]`
`/prioritize kano [list of items]`

## What This Command Does
Default: Uses `rice-prioritization`
Weighted mode: Uses `weighted-scoring`
Kano mode: Uses `kano-model`

Outputs: Ranked list with scores and rationale.
