# /draft-rfp

Draft a comprehensive RFP or respond to one.

## Usage
`/draft-rfp [what you need from a vendor]`
`/respond-rfp [describe the RFP you received]`

## What This Command Does
Uses: `rfp-creation` → complete RFP document or response structure.
