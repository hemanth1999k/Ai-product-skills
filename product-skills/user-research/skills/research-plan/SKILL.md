# Research Planning

## Purpose
Design a rigorous, time-efficient research plan that answers the right questions with the right methods.

## Research Plan Template

### 1. Research Question(s)
Define 1-3 specific questions this research will answer:
- Bad: "What do users think of our product?"
- Good: "Why do users who complete onboarding churn within 30 days?"

Ask: What decision will this research inform? Work backwards from the decision.

### 2. Research Type Selection

| Type | Use When | Methods |
|---|---|---|
| Exploratory | You don't know what you don't know | In-depth interviews, diary studies, ethnography |
| Generative | Defining the problem space | JTBD interviews, contextual inquiry |
| Evaluative | Testing a specific solution | Usability tests, A/B tests, concept tests |
| Descriptive | Quantifying known patterns | Surveys, analytics, cohort analysis |

### 3. Participant Criteria
Define:
- Who are the ideal participants? (role, behavior, segment)
- Inclusion criteria (must-haves)
- Exclusion criteria (who to exclude and why)
- Sample size rationale:
  - Qualitative: 5-8 per segment (saturation)
  - Quantitative: Use power calculation (see ab-test-design skill)

### 4. Method Selection
Match methods to questions:
- "Why" questions → interviews, ethnography
- "How many" questions → surveys, analytics
- "Can users do X" questions → usability tests
- "Which is better" questions → A/B tests

### 5. Research Instruments
What you'll create:
- Discussion guide / interview script
- Survey questionnaire
- Usability test tasks
- Stimulus materials (mockups, prototypes)

### 6. Timeline & Resources
- Participant recruitment: X days
- Study sessions: X days
- Analysis: X days
- Readout: X days
- Total: X weeks

### 7. Analysis Plan
Define upfront:
- How will you analyze qualitative data? (affinity mapping, thematic analysis)
- What quantitative metrics will you calculate?
- How will you present findings? (research report, one-pager, video clips)

## Output Format
- Research brief (1-pager)
- Method recommendation with rationale
- Participant screener criteria
- Timeline
