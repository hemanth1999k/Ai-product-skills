# /brainstorm

Deep creative brainstorm for a product problem or opportunity.

## Usage
`/brainstorm [topic or problem]`

## What This Command Does
Uses `brainstorm-product-ideas` + `product-brainstormer` together for maximum creative coverage.

## Example
`/brainstorm How might we reduce time-to-value for new users in our analytics product?`
